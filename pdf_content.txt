SECTION 3: ABOUT US
Who We Are & Why We Were Built
Section Heading
H2
Built for the Way Africa Does Business
SUBHEADING
Earth Metals was created to solve a problem that every fleet owner, procurement manager, and 
equipment dealer knows too well. The commercial asset market across Africa is fragmented, 
inefficient, and hard to navigate.
Body Copy
Buying or selling a truck, excavator, or fleet of vehicles should not rely on informal contacts, 
manual paperwork, or guesswork around pricing. As industries across Southern Africa grow and  
asset movements increase, the need for a structured, digital alternative has never been greater.
Earth Metals is that alternative. We are a B2B online auction platform built exclusively for 
commercial vehicles, plant equipment, and parts. Our platform replaces offline trading methods 
with a transparent, technology-driven marketplace that gives buyers and sellers the structure, 
reach, and confidence they need to trade at scale.
We serve fleet owners and dealers looking to dispose of assets efficiently, construction and 
mining companies sourcing reliable machinery, logistics operators managing procurement, and 
financial institutions requiring structured disposal processes, all within a single, trusted platform 
across Southern Africa.

SECTION 11: TRACKING & ADS READINESS
Technical Notes for Developer
Google Analytics 4 (GA4)
Install GA4 tracking on all pages. Set up the following conversion events:
•form_submit: triggered on Contact form submission
•register_click: triggered when user clicks Register to Bid or Get Started
•listing_view: triggered when a user views an individual auction listing
•search_performed: triggered when a user searches or filters listings
Meta Pixel (Facebook / Instagram Ads)
Install Meta Pixel on all pages for remarketing and conversion tracking. Standard events to 
configure:
•PageView: all pages
•Lead: on contact form submission
•CompleteRegistration: on successful user registration
•ViewContent: on individual listing views
LinkedIn Insight Tag
Install LinkedIn Insight Tag on all pages to support B2B retargeting and audience building for 
sponsored content and lead generation campaigns. Priority for Earth Metals given its B2B 
audience (fleet managers, procurement, operations).
Google Ads: Conversion Actions
Set up the following conversion actions in Google Ads:
•Contact Form Submission
•Registration Started
•Registration Completed
•Listing Enquiry
Page Speed & Technical SEO
•All images must include descriptive alt text containing relevant keywords (e.g., 
alt="Inspected commercial trucks for auction South Africa")
•Implement structured data (schema.org): Organization, LocalBusiness (x2), WebSite, 
and BreadcrumbList
•Submit XML sitemap to Google Search Console on launch
•Set up canonical URLs to prevent duplicate content issues
•Ensure page load speed below 3 seconds on mobile
•Implement Open Graph tags on all pages for social sharing
CMS / Content Notes
The platform should allow easy future addition of:
•Blog / News section for SEO content marketing (keyword-rich articles on commercial 
vehicle trading, fleet management, market trends)
•Auction Listings page with filter functionality (by asset type, country, price range)
•Individual Listing pages with full inspection report display and bidding interface
•User Dashboard (registered buyers and sellers)
