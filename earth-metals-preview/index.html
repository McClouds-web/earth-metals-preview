About Earth Metals

The Specialist Marketplace for Heavy Assets.

By combining professional inspections with a structured bidding process, we ensure that every transaction is grounded in trust, clarity, and market-driven value. Whether you are disposing of an entire mining fleet or sourcing a single prime mover, Earth Metals provides the tools and the network to trade with confidence.

Why Businesses Choose Earth Metals

In a market where commercial asset transactions have traditionally been slow, informal, and unpredictable, Earth Metals brings structure, speed, and confidence to every transaction.

01 Verified, Inspected Assets

Every vehicle and piece of equipment listed on Earth Metals is professionally inspected before going to auction. Buyers receive accurate condition reports, service history documentation, and transparent asset records, removing the risk and uncertainty of uninspected purchases.

02 Qualified, Verified Participants Only

Earth Metals is a closed, business-to-business marketplace. Buyers and sellers are vetted before participation, ensuring every interaction is with a legitimate, capable counterparty. This eliminates wasted time, unqualified enquiries, and fraudulent activity.

03 Transparent Pricing, No Surprises

Real-time competitive bidding means prices are determined by the market, not by negotiation or guesswork. Both buyer and seller have full visibility throughout the process, with no hidden fees or last-minute changes.

04 Structured, End-to-End Process

From listing to payment and handover, every transaction on Earth Metals follows a clearly defined workflow. This reduces delays, prevents disputes, and ensures both parties have a consistent, reliable experience regardless of asset type or transaction size.

05 Pan-African Reach from Day One

With offices in Johannesburg and Harare and operations serving the wider Southern African region, Earth Metals provides access to a cross-border buyer and seller network that no single local marketplace can match.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about buying and selling on Earth Metals.

Auctions on Earth Metals typically run for 5–10 business days, giving qualified buyers sufficient time to review inspection reports and place competitive bids. Specific auction durations are set per asset.

Every asset undergoes a professional third-party inspection before it is listed. This includes a full condition report, photographic documentation, service history verification, and any known defects clearly disclosed.

We are headquartered in South Africa and Zimbabwe, with operations serving the wider Southern African region including Botswana, Zambia, Mozambique, and Namibia. Our buyer and seller network spans 6+ countries.

Earth Metals is a business-to-business (B2B) platform. Participants must be registered businesses. This ensures all buyers and sellers are qualified, vetted, and capable counterparties — protecting every transaction.

Payment is confirmed and secured before any asset handover takes place. Our structured process includes formal documentation at every stage, eliminating risk for both buyer and seller.

Click "Register" on the platform. You will be guided through our business verification process. Once approved, you will have immediate access to browse current auctions or list your first asset — typically within 48 hours.
